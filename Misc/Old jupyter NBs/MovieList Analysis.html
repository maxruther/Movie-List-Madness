Takeaway: Add Genre data

When I look through this table, a differentiating pattern isn't as glaring as I might've hoped. However, I wonder whether films associated with either the comedy or romance genres might tend to be rated poorer.

Being a fan of comedy, I would be surprised if such a pattern proved determinant. But regardless, adding genre fields to this data appears advantageous.
